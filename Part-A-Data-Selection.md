# Assessment Task 2
## Part A - **Data Selection and Game Attributes**
### <u>Attributes</u>


| Rank  | Attribute                     | Higher or Lower Wins? | Why it was Chosen                                                                                                | Fair / Unfair Considerations                                                                                                    |
| ----- | ----------------------------- | --------------------- | ---------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- | ------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| **1** | **Power (kW)**                | Higher                | Power was chosen because it represents overall vehicle performance and creates strong competition between cards. | Fair because vehicles have clear variation in power. Unfair if high-power cars dominate gameplay too often.                     |
| **2** | **Top Speed (km/h)**          | Higher                | Top speed represents maximum performance and adds excitement and clear comparison between vehicles.              | Fair because performance differences are visible. Unfair if high-speed vehicles consistently dominate rounds.                   |
| **3** | **Fuel Efficiency (L/100km)** | Lower                 | Fuel efficiency adds strategy by allowing efficient vehicles to compete with high-performance vehicles.          | Fair because lower fuel consumption rewards efficiency. Unfair if efficiency becomes more important than performance stats.     |
| **4** | **Year Manufactured**         | Higher                | Year was selected because newer vehicles often include improved technology and design features.                  | Fair as newer models can have advantages. Unfair because older high-performance vehicles may be disadvantaged.                  |
| **5** | **Price (AUD)**               | Higher                | Price reflects market value and perceived quality, adding another comparison category.                           | Fair when balanced across other attributes. Unfair if expensive vehicles consistently win due to inflated values.               |
| **6** | **Fuel Capacity (L)**         | Higher                | Fuel capacity supports more practical vehicles and adds variety beyond performance-based stats.                  | Fair because it encourages diversity in gameplay. Unfair if players ignore practical attributes in favour of performance stats. |
